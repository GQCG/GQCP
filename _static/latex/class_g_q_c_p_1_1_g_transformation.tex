\subsubsection*{template$<$typename \+\_\+\+Scalar$>$\newline
class GQCP\+::\+GTransformation$<$ \+\_\+\+Scalar $>$}
A \textquotesingle{}general\textquotesingle{} basis transformation, i.\+e. a general, full-\/spinor basis transformation where the transformation mixes the alpha-\/ and beta components of the two-\/component spinors.

In general, we adopt the convention outlined in (\href{https://gqcg-res.github.io/knowdes/spinor-transformations.html}{\texttt{ https\+://gqcg-\/res.\+github.\+io/knowdes/spinor-\/transformations.\+html}}), where the new orbitals\textquotesingle{} coefficients can be found in the respective {\bfseries{column}} of the related transformation matrix.


\begin{DoxyTemplParams}{Template Parameters}
{\em \+\_\+\+Scalar} & The scalar type used for a transformation coefficient\+: real or complex. \\
