A general spinor basis, i.\+e. a spinor basis without any restrictions on the expansion of the alpha and beta components of the spinors in terms of the underlying (possibly different) scalar bases.


\begin{DoxyTemplParams}{Template Parameters}
{\em \+\_\+\+Expansion\+Scalar} & The scalar type used to represent an expansion coefficient of the spinors in the underlying scalar orbitals\+: real or complex. \\
\hline
{\em \+\_\+\+Shell} & The type of shell the underlying scalar bases contain.\\
\hline
\end{DoxyTemplParams}
\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}

\end{DoxyNote}
