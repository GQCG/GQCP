Construct a {\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{class_g_q_c_p_1_1_simple_spin_orbital_basis}{Simple\+Spin\+Orbital\+Basis}}} with an underlying scalar basis that is made by placing shells corresponding to the basisset specification on every nucleus of the molecule. The resulting spin-\/orbital basis then corresponds to the (non-\/orthogonal) atomic spin-\/orbitals (A\+Os).
